Speaker For The Dead

Orson Scott Card is a fairly famous author who was born in Richland, Washington and is very well known for his Ender’s Game series. In the second book in the series, Speaker for the Dead, Ender must go on an adventure in a colony on the planet of Lusitania to solve several alien-caused murders. The major theme of good and evil in this story is that cultures usually find what is unusual and misunderstood to them to be evil while they find what is familiar to their culture to be good.
3000 years have passed since the ending of Ender’s Game and the destruction of the buggers. Ender is now a Speaker for the Dead, traveling across the galaxy to whichever planet requests his speaking services. One day, he is called by a young 13 year old girl named Novinha to arrive at the Milagre colony on the planet of Lusitania to speak the death of Xenobiologist named Pipo, who seemed to have been mercilessly slaughtered by aliens that he was assigned to study. When he arrives at Lusitania after 22 years of space travel, things in the colony have gotten much worse. Several more deaths have occurred at the hands of the aliens, colloquially named “piggies” by the colonists. These deaths included humans and piggies, both being killed the same way; with their bodies torn open and entrails being spread about the ground. Through investigation during his time in the colony, Ender is eventually able to solve the great mystery of these deaths. The piggies’ murders, which the humans believed to be completely malevolent and evil, were actually a ritual of great honor in the piggy culture. With this answer finally discovered, humans and piggies are able to make a treaty of mutual respect and live together in harmony.

Orson Scott Card

Orson Scott Card was born in Richland, Washington on August 24,1951. During his childhood, he spent much of his time at the public library reading plenty of books from a diversity of subjects, including a relatively new type of novel known as science fiction. He eventually went to college at BYU and studied to be theater major, writing plays and such as a theater student. Several years after he graduated college, Card moved with his family to Utah, Indiana, and finally to North Carolina where he began writing his Ender’s Game series. His works in writing eventually propelled him to become a famous author.

Analysis

The common theme of good and evil in this book is that people usually find what is familiar to them and their culture to be good, and what is foreign and not understood to be evil.The main conflict in this story was solving the murders of Pipo and all the others who were brutally murdered by the piggies. Nobody ever once considered that this ritualistic murder was actually an honor that meant they held the victim in very high respect. Because this sort of ritual was unfamiliar to the culture of the colonists on Lusitania, they instantly believed this was an evil act that was done with malicious intent. But once they dug deeper into understanding what had brought on this atrocity towards their people, the discovered that the piggies did not feel that it was atrocity at all. For the piggies’ culture, they did that to the individuals who had reached the pinnacle of society. Once they were ritualistically murdered, the piggies believed that the victims became the trees of the forest, who these aliens believed to harness the souls of their most venerated ancestors. Once these two cultures met each other and totally explained how their societies worked, they were able to completely appreciate each other for being good because they they now finally understood each other. This theme of all things unfamiliar being evil was also present during the bugger wars of Ender’s Game. The humans didn’t understand that the buggers were trying to make peace with them due to the buggers’ unfamiliar culture and appearances, causing the humans to go to war with the buggers and eradicate them all. If they would have taken the time to understand the bugger culture before going to war with them, then they would have realized that the aliens were a peaceful group who just wanted to coexist with the humans.
